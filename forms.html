What Is A Form?

Now that's a good question, slugger. Back at the dawn of humanity, mankind invented fire, and was thus elevated above the animals. Since the era of that great invention, there has been only one which could rival its importance, its power , its magisty... the form... Just as fire illuminated the world of primitive man, forms illuminated the minds of modern man, elevating him once again. It is forms that seperate the civilized from the savage. They are the mighty sword of order that cuts through the chaos. They are weapons in the hands of heros to fight against the darkness and disorder that lurks in every unmapped corner of the infosphere. The Ministry of Information asks that you help it in this most holy of wars by using these forms properly. They are powerful tools, and they demand not just your admiration, but your respect.
